About the Course

This class will teach you how to cast charms on a multitude of objects. Charms are spells that alter an object without changing its essential nature. For example, given a teapot, a charm could make it tapdance across your desk!

Syllabus

  • Charm Basics 9/3
  • Charm History 9/20
  • How to make inanimate objects move laterally 10/5
  • How to make inanimate objects fly 11/7
  • How to make objects dissappear 12/18
  • How to make objects dance 1/17
  • How to make objects destroy itself 3/15
