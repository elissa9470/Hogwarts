Offered Courses

Potions

Ever wondered the effects of Amortentia? Have you wanted to create and master the Draught of Peace? Then Potions is the perfect course for you. Discover how to create and utilize countless amounts of potions.

Transfiguration

In this course, some of our best instructors will teach you how to transform into what you desire. Turn into a bird to soar and fly, turn into a mouse to sneak around in the dark. But beware, this takes lots of practice and effort.

Defense Against the Dark Arts

Learn basic and advanced methods of self defense. This is a required course that will teach students how to avoid getting hurt, and precautions they should take to keep their defense skills sharp.

Flying

This class will teach students how to maneuver and master their flying skills as well as the game of quidditch.
