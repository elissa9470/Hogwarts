Syllubus

  1. Quarter One: We will be learning basic background knowledge on potions, and when to use them.
  2. Quarter Two: We will learn about the basic defense spells that will aid students in combat and emergency situations. Safety will be the biggest concern.
  3. Quarter Three: This will be the first time actually using potions. It is important to know the basics before testing and using real potions. Make sure to bring required items.
  4. Quarter Four: We will combine potions with defense and learn skills that will be used in the world. We will also be conducting final exams and assesments to test student readiness and mastery.
